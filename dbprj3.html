Bikeshare AnalyticsSystem

Analysing bikeshare data for profits

Collected New York and Boston City bike usage data from bikeshares for data visualisation and exploration using SparkSQL to find and exploit the leverage points to gain maximum profit.

Queried the data to get simple statistics such as riders making maximum trips, total subscribers of one bikeshare company, peak riding hours in a day and average statistics of a trip.

Complex queries such as finding the locations where maximum round trips occured were helpful in increasing profits for the bikeshare company.
